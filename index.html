About Me

My introduction

Muhammad Masih, known as the "Young Diplomat" in Iraq and the Kurdistan Region, was born in exile in 1997 after his family fled to Iran following chemical bombings in 1988. He earned a degree in International Relations from Soran University, where he actively engaged in youth leadership, charity work, and academic initiatives. Elected as Ambassador of Peace by the Global Peace Chain in 2019, and later honored as Best Ambassador in 2022, Muhammad has represented Iraq in major forums like the Paris Peace Forum. As CEO of Bazan Institute, he collaborates with local and international organizations on peace, coexistence, and human rights. Muhammad believes in empowering youth to create hope and drive change, even in challenging circumstances.
